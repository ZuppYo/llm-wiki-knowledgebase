# Philips AUT8940 Features and Specifications

Compiled from [Philips AUT8940 RO Water Filter 101](../Topics/philips-aut8940-ro-water-filter-101.md).

## System overview

- **Type:** Tankless direct-flow RO under-sink unit (no storage tank or pressure tank).
- **Technology:** Aquaporin (AQP) RO membrane — marketing claims **110+ contaminant types** including bacteria, viruses, chlorine, heavy metals, PFAS.
- **Remineralization:** Adds minerals back after RO (e.g. calcium, magnesium, potassium) so drinking water is not flat/bitter.
- **FreshPro:** Auto-flushes membrane with purified water on startup (~18 s per source).
- **Recovery:** Up to **~70%** product water vs **~30%** drain (better than typical 50/50 RO waste ratios cited in review).

## Dual output modes (one faucet)

| Mode | Path | Flow (review) | Use |
|------|------|---------------|-----|
| **RO** | Full RO + AQP | ~1.05 L/min | Drinking |
| **MF** | Micro-filtration only (AUT615 / 4-in-1) | ~2 L/min | Rinsing produce, general sink use |

Faucet has **two hoses** (blue = RO, green = MF) and a selector.

## Filters (AUT8940)

| Code | Role | Life (source) |
|------|------|---------------|
| **AUT615** (4-in-1) | Composite / MF path | ~1 year |
| **AUT620 R400** | RO membrane (AQP) | Up to ~3 years / ~11,000 L rated |

**QuickTwist** bayonet — twist to remove/install.

## AUT8990 vs AUT8940 (mentioned in review)

| | AUT8940 | AUT8990 |
|---|---------|---------|
| GPD | 400 | 800 |
| RO flow | ~1.05 L/min | ~2 L/min |
| Size | Similar cabinet | Similar |
| Power | Lower | Higher |
| Price band (review) | ~mid-10k THB on promo | ~10k+ to high teens THB |
| Extras | Touch panel on unit | Black finish; faucet/display with TDS monitoring |

Review suggests AUT8940 is enough for typical **4–5 person** household daily drinking; AUT8990 for heavy continuous use.
