# Rubberwood Spray Painting 101

End-to-end spray-finish workflow for **rubberwood plywood** (ไม้อัดยาง), from [Indy Woodworking Thailand](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3bFrM97xHSk) Ep.19 (2020). Aimed at beginners doing household or small-shop furniture and DIY color work.

**Compiled workflow:** [Rubberwood spray painting workflow](../Concepts/rubberwood-spray-painting-workflow.md)

**Source caveat:** The Raw transcript is auto-generated; product names and some steps are approximate. Verify against the video before buying materials or quoting specs.

**Scope from source:** Putty fill → sand → sealer/primer rounds → defect repair → top coat (matte white demo) → optional matte lacquer and light touch-up sand. Light/white final colors use a **white primer** path; dark opaque colors follow a different primer choice (mentioned but not fully demoed).
